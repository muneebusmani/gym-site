Our Journey

From a Small Gym to a Fitness Empire

PowerFit was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to create a welcoming space where everyone, regardless of their fitness level, could work towards becoming their best self.

What started as a single 2,000 sq ft facility has grown into a 50,000 sq ft state-of-the-art fitness center, equipped with the latest technology and staffed by industry-leading professionals.

Today, we're proud to serve over 5,000 active members and have helped countless individuals achieve their fitness dreams.
